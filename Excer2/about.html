The Youth Alliance For Change

The Youth Alliance For Change remains committed to empowering young people, promoting peace, protecting human rights, and fostering sustainable development in our communities. We believe that through unity, advocacy, and leadership, the voices of the youth can inspire meaningful change and create a brighter future for all.

Our Mission

To equip young people with the skills, opportunities, and platforms they need to become agents of positive change in their communities.

Vision & Values

Vision: A future where empowered youth create peaceful and prosperous communities.

Integrity Inclusiveness Creativity Leadership Unity

Objectives

  1. Environment & Sustainability
  2. Civic Engagement & Leadership
  3. advocacy & Human Rights
  4. Community Impact Goals
  5. Peacebuilding & Conflict Resolution
  6. Mental Health & Well-Being

Birth Of The Organization

The Organization was founded on the 5th of August 2025 and this is a youth-led Organization.

Impact

We support community development, education programs, and youth-led initiatives that create lasting change.
